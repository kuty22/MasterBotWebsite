What is MasterBot ?

MasterBot is a web platform which will allow you to invest without minimal amount on
several MasterNode of CryptoCurrency like Dash, Neo, Crown, etc.


But what is a MasterNode in the Crypto-World ?

A MasterNode is simply an amount of cryptocurrency needed for
protect the network of this cryptocurrency, then like a proof of work we can pretend to reward.
